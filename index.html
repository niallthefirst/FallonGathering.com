What

The Fallon Gathering 2013 is an event which hopes to reunite the entire Fallon diaspora residing both in Ireland and throughout the world. The Gathering Ireland is global event setup by the Irish tourist board, Bordfailte.

When

At this point the Fallon Gathering is planned for the last weekend of September 2013, keep and eye on Facebook or the Gathering website.

Where

The Fallon Gathering event will take place in Rahara village, Co.Roscommon.

Who

The Fallon Gathering is open to all who have some relationship to the Fallon Clan, keep and eye on Facebook or the Gathering website.
