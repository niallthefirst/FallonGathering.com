September 28th , 2013 Fallon Gathering Ireland 2013 in Dysart, Co.Roscommon, Ireland
The Fallon Gathering 2013 is an event which hopes to reunite the entire Fallon diaspora residing both in Ireland and throughout the world. http://www.fallongathering.com Categories: thegatheringireland, fallon

PROGRAMME OF ACTIVITY

More information on Saturday's Summer School: The Fallon Gathering Summer School will commence at 10.30 a.m. in Dysart Community Centre. Distinguished academic Dr. Nollaig Ó Muraíle, senior lecturer and Head of the Irish Department in NUI, Galway, and well-known local historian William Gacquin will be the keynote speakers.

Dr Ó Muraíle, who is one of the leading authorities on Irish genealogies, will talk about the history of the Fallon Clan in medieval and early modern times. The history of the O’Fallon Clan can be traced to the ancestral lands of Clan Uadach in the parishes of Dysart and Cam in South Roscommon. Mr Gacquin will speak about the story of the Fallon clan after medieval times.

Light refreshments will be provided by 12.30 p.m. and this will be followed by a tour of the historic sites associated with the Fallon Clan including the Fallon Clan Castle, the ancient cemetery in Dysart and Cloonagh House which was the seat of the O’Fallon Clan and in the 18th century it was the base of Rev. James O'Fallon who was the Bishop of Elphin from 1756 to 1786. After the tour of the historic sites a visit will take place to Derryglad Folk Museum. An interlude will follow to allow people to watch the All-Ireland hurling final replay if they wish before the centrepiece event of the weekend, the Fallon Clan Banquet, will take place in the Abbey Hotel at 8.30 p.m.
